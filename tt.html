Tourist Visa

An Australia Tourist Visa is a type of non-immigrant visa. It allows foreign nationals to visit Australia for a short time period.
When you apply for a tourist visa, you cannot work for any business or sell services/goods in Australia.
People intending to informal study or training for less than 3 months, may also apply for a Visitor visa.
Parents of Australian citizens and permanent residents are able to apply for Visitor visas to visit Australia for longer.

Foreign nationals must obtain a valid Australian visa or travel permit to enter the country. The Australian government has implemented an electronic visa system that allows travellers to obtain their visas online.

You must have sufficient funds to support yourself.
You must display intent to return to your home country.
You must display the purpose of the visit.
You must show that you are in good health.
You must display that you have good character.
You must show proof that you have no criminal records.
You must not have any debts to the Australian government.
You must not accept or seek any jobs.
You cannot study using this visa.
You must follow the laws of the country.

Documentation for an Australia Tourist Visa
Valid passport
Old passports (if any)
Recent photographs (as per the requirements)
Completed visa application form (Form 1419)
Documents showing your purpose for visit
Proof of funds
Bank statements (past six months)
Income tax returns (past three years)
Property investments (if any)
Fixed deposits (if any)
Medical test results
X-ray examinations
Personal cover letter
Travel Insurance
Proofs of Accommodation

Application Process for an Australia Visit Visa
Step 1: You must choose a suitable subclass.
Step 2: Submit the necessary documents.
Step 3: Fill out the visa application form.
Step 4: Pay the required visa fees.
Step 5: Arrange an appointment for submitting the application form.
Step 6: If you meet the eligibility criteria, you will get a tourist visa.
