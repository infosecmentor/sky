Recent Works

Business Schengen Visa

A business visa is an authorization of the designated country/countries of destination within the Schengen Area issued to the citizens of countries that have mandatory visa requirements from the Schengen Zone with the sole purpose of doing business in the EU

Schengen Tourist Visa

A Tourist Schengen Visa permits third-country nationals to enter the Schengen Area for a maximum of 90 days within a 6-month period, for the purpose of travelling or visiting Europe for pleasure.

Student Schengen Visa

The Student Schengen Visa is a short-term Europe study visa. As a result, the European student visa allows a student to stay in Schengen Zone member countries for three months. If your course has a longer duration, you will need to apply for a long-stay study visa for that country.

Australia Tourist Visa

An Australia Tourist Visa is a type of non-immigrant visa. It allows foreign nationals to visit Australia for a short time period.

Australia Business Visa

For successful business owners, investors and senior executives who wish to settle in Australia, the Australian Business Visa Program is an encouraging pathway towards conducting your business opportunities in Australia

Australia Student Visa

A student visa is a short-term visa that allows you to study in Australia for the duration of your course. The courses range from general English to university-level courses. In most cases, as an Australian International student, you will be permitted to work a maximum of 40 hours per fortnight or 20 hours per week.
